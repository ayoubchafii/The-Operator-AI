# My Business — Context File

Fill out the sections that apply to you. Skip what doesn't. Be honest, not aspirational. Short answers beat long ones. The advisor uses this to give you tailored advice instead of generic frameworks.

If you don't have a business yet, delete this file. The advisor will give you general advice instead.

---

## What I sell

One sentence. Plain language. Not a pitch.

> Example: WhatsApp AI receptionist for dental clinics that books appointments in Arabic, English, and French.

---

## Who I sell it to

Specific. Not "small business owners." Not "people who need help." A real, narrow buyer.

> Example: Dental clinic owners in Saudi Arabia and UAE with 1-3 locations and at least one full-time receptionist.

---

## What I charge

Price, structure (one-time / monthly / per-result), and what they get for that price.

> Example: $497/month per clinic. Includes the AI receptionist, Google Calendar booking, Make.com workflows, Arabic/English/French handling, and a setup call.

---

## How they buy

What's the path from "stranger" to "customer"? Cold DM → demo call → signed contract? Ad → landing page → checkout? Be literal.

> Example: Cold outreach on Instagram → 15-minute demo → 7-day free trial → signed monthly contract.

---

## How I currently get leads

Which Core Four channels are you actively using? Volume per week? What's working, what isn't?

> Example: Cold Instagram DMs (200/week, ~3% reply rate). No paid ads, no content, no warm outreach yet.

## What I'm stuck on

The real problem, in your own words. Not a framework label. Just: what's not working that you wish was?

> Example: I get demo calls but they ghost after. I'm not sure if it's the price, the demo itself, or the type of clinic I'm targeting.

---

## What I've already tried

Briefly — what have you done in the last 30-60 days to fix the stuck thing? What changed, what didn't?

> Example: Lowered price from $700 to $497, no change in close rate. Added Arabic-language demo video, slight bump in replies.

---

## Constraints I can't easily change

Money, time, language, geography, regulations, team size, technical skill — what's actually fixed in your world right now?

> Example: Solo founder, ~$2,000/month to spend on tools+ads, building this in parallel with university classes, based in Morocco selling to Gulf.

---

## Goals — next 90 days

What does success look like in three months? Specific, measurable.

> Example: 10 paying clinics at $497/month = $5,000 MRR. Working sales process I can hand off to one VA.

---

## Anything else the advisor should know

Anything specific to your situation that doesn't fit above. Founder background, related side projects, weird quirks of your market, past businesses, etc. Keep it relevant.
