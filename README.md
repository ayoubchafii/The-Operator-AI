## 🚀 Absolute Beginner's Setup Guide

You will need a **Claude Pro** account to use the "Projects" feature. Setup takes less than 3 minutes.

### Step 1: Create the Project Space
1. Log into your Claude account.
2. Look at the bottom left corner of your screen and click on **Projects**.
3. Click the **Create Project** button and name it "$100M Advisor".

### Step 2: Upload the Brain (Knowledge Base)
1. Download the `knowledge-base` folder from this GitHub repository to your computer.
2. Inside your new Claude Project, look for the **Project Knowledge** section.
3. Drag and drop all the `.md` files from that folder into this section.

### Step 3: Give the AI its Instructions
1. Open the `system-prompt.md` file from this repository and copy all the text.
2. Inside your Claude Project, click **Edit Instructions** (usually near the top).
3. Paste the text and save.

### Step 4: Add Your Business Context (Crucial)
1. Open the `knowledge-base/00-my-business-TEMPLATE.md` file. 
2. Fill it out with your actual business numbers, what you sell, and where you are stuck. 
3. Upload your completed version into the chat before asking your first question. *This is the secret to getting mind-blowing advice.*
